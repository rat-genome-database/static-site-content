Name Pgk1
Description phosphoglycerate kinase 1
Notes The protein encoded by this gene is a glycolytic enzyme that catalyzes the conversion of 1,3-diphosphoglycerate to 3-phosphoglycerate. The encoded protein may also act as a cofactor for polymerase alpha. A pseudogene of this gene has been found on the X-chromosome and another on chromosome 19.
