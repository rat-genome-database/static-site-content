Description coproporphyrinogen oxidase
Notes Coproporphyrinogen oxidase (EC 1.3.3.3) is the sixth enzyme of the heme biosynthetic pathway. This soluble protein is localized in the intermembrane space of mitochondria and catalyzes the stepwise oxidative decarboxylation of the heme precursor coproporphyrinogen III to protoporphyrinogen IX via the tricarboxylic intermediate harderoporphyrinogen.[supplied by OMIM]
