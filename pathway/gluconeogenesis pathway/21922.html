Name G6pc3
Description glucose 6 phosphatase, catalytic, 3
Notes Glucose-6-phosphatase (G6Pase) is located in the endoplasmic reticulum (ER) and catalyzes hydrolysis of G6P to glucose and phosphate, the last step of the gluconeogenic and glycogenolytic pathways. G6PC3 is a ubiquitously expressed G6Pase catalytic subunit (Martin et al., 2002 [PubMed 12370122]; Guionie et al., 2003 [PubMed 12965222]).[supplied by OMIM]
