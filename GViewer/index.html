View Annotations using Flash GViewer

You can use this form to enter or upload your own annotations for display on GViewer.

There are three steps in the process:

  1. Enter or upload your annotation data
  2. Configure the GViewer display options
  3. Create the GViewer display

An explanation of the annotation data format is available here. If you wish to just give the GViewer a try, copy the sample Rat annotation data from this file, paste it into the annotation data field, leave all the other options at their default settings and click the Visualize Annotations button.
