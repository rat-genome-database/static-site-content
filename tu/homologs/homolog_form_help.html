Species

Choose the species of the desired homolog

 

Homolog Symbol/Alias/Keyword

Enter a homolog symbol, alias or keyword to search for. The asterisk '*' is an accepted wildcard, in addition various other options are available to refine the search:

 

Chromosome

Restrict the returned homologs to ones mapped to a particular chromosome. Excludes homologs that are unmapped or mapped to a different chromosome

Search

This option allows the restriction of the fields to be searched and hence the scope of the returned results. The options are:

  • current symbol (narrow search)
  • current symbol and name
  • current and withdrawn symbols and names
  • current and withdrawn symbols, names and aliases (broadest search).

Limit to homologs with known Homologs

Restrict the returned homologs to ones which have a known homolog listed in RGD (from any organism) .

Results ordered by

  • Symbol - sorts returned results alphabetically by homolog symbol
  • Name - sorts returned results alphabetically by homolog name
  • Chromosome - sorts returned results by chromosome and then alphabetically by homolog symbol

Results per page

Defines the number of hits that are returned on each results page, the more results displayed, the longer the page and the longer it will take to download.
