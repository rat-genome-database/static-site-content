RGD Contents

RGD focuses on rat genetic and genomic information and contains data on the following objects:

 

Object   Description
Genes [Definition] a physical unit for transcription- symbol, name, product, function, role, chromosome location, Gene Ontology and disease.
Strains [Definition] inbred rat strains- name, origin, genetic markers, and detailed information including coat color, drug and chemicals and physiology.
QTL [Definition] Quantitative Trait Loci- symbols, names and aliases mainly of diseases models including diabetes, arthritis, and blood pressure.
SSLPs [Definition] Simple Sequence Length Polymorphism- name, expected size, primers sequences, cloned sequences.
ESTs [Definition] Expressed Sequence Tags- symbol, alias, associated genes, associated sequences and primer pairs
Maps [Definition] genetic and radiation hybrid maps- map length, number of framework and placement markers.
Sequences [Definition] currently limited to SSLP cloned and primer sequences, EST clone and primer sequences.
References [Definition]

literature for manually curated or bulk-exchanged RGD information.
