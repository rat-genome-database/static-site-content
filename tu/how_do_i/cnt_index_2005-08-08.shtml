How Do I use RGD to address my scientific problem?

RGD is a collection of data, tools, and other web resources. This section shows you how they can be linked to answer common scientific problems. Links are provided to the more detailed help documents for individual tools and data.

Areas currently covered: Genetic Mapping, Genomics, Sequences, Ontology new

  • Information on the types of data stored in RGD can be found on the What is in RGD section.
  • The tools and searching functions of RGD are covered in the Tools section.
  • A glossary of terms and definitions is also available
