The Rat Radiation Hybrid Panel is a 106 hybrid panel. More information is available from Research Genetics. The Rat RH framework was generated using hybrids 1 through 100.

The RH vectors should consist of 0's, 1', or 2's from hybrids # 1-106 in that order. In the RH vectors, 0 represents negative, 1 positive and 2 ambiguous or missing data. If you have missed hybrids, please add 2's for those missing hybrids. If you have vectors with length shorter than 106, the tool will add 2's at the end of your vector.

Data Format - The data should be arranged in the following format:

atp1a3 0010000010000000000001100010000000000000000010000001001001000200100001010000000011010011000101222222
atp4a 0001000100000000000001120010200000000000000000000001001000000110100010010000000011010001000201222222
cgrat10 2011101010000001110201100000000000210210000010101121001100120000001000110001201100000020020000010001

The data consists of 2 columns delimited by a tab, space or comma: the first is the marker name which is limited to 11 characters; the second is the RH vector string. Any columns beyond the first two will be discarded.
